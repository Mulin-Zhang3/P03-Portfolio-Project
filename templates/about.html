About Me

Focus

I focus on game design, visual storytelling, and interactive experiences, with a strong interest in scene design and narrative development. I enjoy exploring how visual elements and story can work together to shape emotion.

Design Approach

I also enjoy gameplay design, especially testing mechanics, improving systems, and iterating on rules. Through my projects, I explore how visuals and interaction can come together to create engaging experiences for players and audiences.

Future Goals

In the future, I hope to work in the game or animation industry. I am particularly interested in roles related to game design, scene direction, performance, and story driven creative work.
