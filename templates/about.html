About Me

I am a student interested in game design, visual storytelling, and interactive experiences. I am especially interested in scene design and narrative development, and I enjoy thinking about how visual elements and story can work together to shape emotion.

I am also interested in gameplay design, especially testing mechanics, improving systems, and iterating on rules. Through my projects, I want to explore how both visuals and interaction can create engaging experiences for players and audiences.

In the future, I hope to work in the game or animation industry. I am particularly interested in roles related to game design, performance or scene direction, and story-driven creative work.
